HISTORY


Dan

Jon
Band

The strange power of musical destiny may never be fully understood in regards to the four members of Supersonic Audio Chronic.

Turkish string charmer Jon touched down in the Carolinas before importing himself to the Mile High City. He comes bearing the gifts of melodic riffs, old world wisdom and an alluring European suaveness.

Frontman and lyrical expressionist Daniel, along with drummer Josh also migrated west shortly thereafter.

Transplants who paid their dues and became men in the heart of Dixie, these guys have years of stage & studio experience in styles ranging from metal to classic blues.

Supersonic Audio Chronic – blending all the joys and sorrows, the unbelievable and the everyday, hard rock, funk rock, jam rock and alotta soul for your earhole!
